Never Pour Oil Down A Drain or Garbage Can!

Oil should always be disposed in a glass jar that is surrounded by a bag and lastly placed in a trash can. You could also reuse cooking oil but thats kind of gross.

Dangers from disposing oil correctly: You could pollute 1,000,000 gallons of water. Also, you clog drains by disposing oil in drains and if you dispose hot oil in the trash, that will start a huge fire.
