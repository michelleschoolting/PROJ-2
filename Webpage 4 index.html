Burnt Firewood can be recycled and pollutes the area when disposed incorrectly!

You should always dispose ashes by first, putting water on the burnt ashes and lastly, disposing them in a garbage tash can. You can ALSO recycle burnt firewood ashes by putting them in ur garden to repel pests like snails and slugs, enrichen soil, melt ice, and even shine silver.
